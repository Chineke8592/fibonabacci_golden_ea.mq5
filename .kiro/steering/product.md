# Product Overview

Financial analysis system for multi-timeframe forex trading analysis with pip-based interval tracking.

## Purpose
- Multi-timeframe analysis across different chart periods (M1, M5, M15, H1, H4, D1)
- Track price movements in 20-30 pip intervals for entry/exit signals
- Provide comprehensive market analysis for forex pairs
- Generate trading insights based on timeframe convergence

## Key Requirements
- Real-time data processing for multiple currency pairs
- Low-latency analysis for scalping and day trading strategies
- Accurate pip calculation across different forex pairs
- Historical data storage and backtesting capabilities
- Risk management and position sizing calculations

## Business Context
- Target forex traders and financial analysts
- Focus on short to medium-term trading strategies
- Competitive advantage through multi-timeframe correlation analysis
- Success measured by signal accuracy and profitability metrics
